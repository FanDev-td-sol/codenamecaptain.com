Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a bug in one of your games?

To report a bug, please email our support team at support@codenamecaptain.com with the subject line "Bug Report: [Game Name]". Include details about the issue, steps to reproduce it, and if possible, screenshots or videos of the bug in action. Our QA team will investigate and respond promptly.

Are you hiring game developers?

We're always looking for talented individuals to join our team! Check our Careers page for current openings, or send your resume and portfolio to careers@codenamecaptain.com. We particularly value creativity, problem-solving skills, and a passion for creating engaging player experiences.

Do you offer refunds for game purchases?

Yes, we offer refunds within 14 days of purchase if you've played less than 2 hours of the game. To request a refund, please contact our support team with your order number and reason for the refund. Please note that special promotional items or in-game consumables may not be eligible for refunds.

Can I become a beta tester for upcoming games?

Absolutely! We regularly invite players to participate in beta testing. Sign up for our newsletter to receive announcements about beta testing opportunities, or join our Discord server where we post exclusive beta access invitations for community members.
