The Story

Step into the shoes of Detective Alex Morgan, a brilliant but troubled investigator with a knack for solving the most perplexing cases. In Detective Mystery, you'll navigate through the shadowy streets of Blackwood City, a metropolis where corruption runs deep and secrets lurk around every corner.

Each case presents a unique challenge, from tracking down elusive serial killers to uncovering elaborate corporate conspiracies. Your decisions will shape the narrative, leading to multiple possible outcomes and endings.

Gameplay Features

  • Evidence Collection Scour crime scenes for clues, photograph evidence, and build your case file.
  • Interrogation System Question suspects using psychological tactics, catching them in lies or contradictions.
  • Detective Board Connect evidence, suspects, and motives on your interactive case board.
  • Reputation System Your actions affect how colleagues, criminals, and the public perceive you.

The Story

Dive into the enchanting underwater world of Oceania as Finn, a young adventurous fish with dreams bigger than the ocean itself. After discovering an ancient map in his grandfather's sunken ship, Finn embarks on a quest to find the legendary Crystal Trident, a powerful artifact said to grant its wielder control over the seas.

Your journey will take you through vibrant coral reefs, mysterious deep-sea trenches, abandoned shipwrecks, and the treacherous waters of the Volcanic Vents. Along the way, you'll befriend colorful marine creatures who will aid you in your quest while facing off against the nefarious Captain Blackfin and his shark gang.

Gameplay Features

  • Swimming Mechanics Master fluid swimming controls to navigate through tight spaces and strong currents.
  • School Formations Recruit fish allies to form protective schools with unique formation abilities.
  • Ecosystem Interaction Influence the underwater ecosystem by planting coral, cleaning pollution, and more.
  • Bubble Communication Use bubble messages to communicate with other sea creatures and unlock new areas.

The Story

In the golden age of piracy, you are Captain James "Red Eye" Blackwood, a once-respected naval officer framed for treason and left to die on a deserted island. Rescued by a crew of outcasts and misfits, you've sworn vengeance against the corrupt Governor Walton and his East India Trading Company allies.

Your path to redemption lies in finding the seven pieces of the legendary Star of Poseidon, a mystical artifact that can control the very weather itself. Sail the treacherous Caribbean waters, build your reputation from shipwrecked nobody to feared pirate lord, and assemble a crew of the most notorious scallywags ever to sail the seven seas.

Gameplay Features

  • Ship Combat Master the art of naval warfare with cannons, boarding actions, and tactical maneuvers.
  • Treasure Hunting Decode ancient maps, solve island puzzles, and dig for buried treasure.
  • Crew Management Recruit, train, and manage your pirate crew, each with unique skills and stories.
  • Port Trading Trade exotic goods, negotiate with merchants, and manipulate the Caribbean economy.
