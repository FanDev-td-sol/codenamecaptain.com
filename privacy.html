Information We Collect

2.1. Personal Information

Personal information is data that can identify you directly or indirectly. Examples include:

  • Your name

  • Username or player name
  • Information provided through in-game communications or our contact forms

2.2. Non-Personal Information

Non-personal information includes technical details of your interaction with our services, such as:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device details (e.g., mobile or desktop)

  • IP address
  • Viewed pages on our website
  • In-game services (e.g., scores, achievements)

Use of Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • Provision and maintenance of our free social games.
